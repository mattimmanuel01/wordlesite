How to Play

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries. After each guess, the tiles will flip to reveal color-coded clues:

Green — correct letter, correct position.
Yellow — correct letter, wrong position.
Gray — letter is not in the word.

Levels

Each word is a level. Complete a level to unlock the next one. Your progress is saved automatically — pick up right where you left off.

My progress was lost

Game progress is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will reset your progress. Unfortunately, there is no way to recover lost data as nothing is stored on a server.
